Time: 30 minutes
Portion: Serves 12
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:

  • 30g milk chocolate
  • 85g dark chocolate
  • 120g sugar
  • 140g butter
  • 70g flour
  • 35g cocoa powder
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • Chocolate for topping (optional)

Instructions:

Melt the butter, milk, and dark chocolate on low gas. Donâ€™t overheat. Meanwhile, whisk the eggs and sugar. Pour the chocolate and butter mix into the eggs and sugar. Add flour, cocoa powder, and salt and mix. Lastly, add chocolate on top. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Let it cool, cut, and serve. Enjoy!
