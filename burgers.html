Time: 40 minutes
Portion: Serves 4
Difficulty: Easy

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • Burger breads
  • 750g beef
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonesse

Instructions:

Add salt and black pepper to beef. Mix. Then divide it into 4 parts (200-250g each) and put it in the fridge until we make other stuff. On a pan fry onions on low gas. After that fry bread in butter until it's crunchy and finally beef on medium gas. Leave it for 15 minutes on each side. After half an hour in total, put cheddar cheese on top for 1 minute. Then make a sauce by mixing mayonesse, ketchup and onion. Put everything together and enjoy!
