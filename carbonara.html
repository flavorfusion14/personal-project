Time: 30 minutes
Portion: Serves 3
Difficulty: Medium

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • 300g pasta
  • 20g black pepper
  • 60g bacon
  • 60g parmesan
  • One egg
  • 3 egg yolks

Instructions:

Boil the pasta in salted water for 10-12 minutes. Mix parmesan and eggs (one whole egg, and 3 egg yolks). While the pasta is boiling, fry the bacon for 7-8 minutes on a pan with olive oil on medium gas. Then put black pepper on the same pan, add pasta water, and then add pasta. Then add pasta water to the cheese+egg mix, and finally add that sauce to the pasta. Lastly, add bacon and serve. Enjoy!
