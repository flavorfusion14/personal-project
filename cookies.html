Time: 35 minutes
Portion: Serves 25
Difficulty: Medium

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

Dough

  • 250g butter
  • 1 vanilla
  • 75g white sugar
  • 75g brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 500g flour
  • Pinch of baking soda
  • 30ml milk

Cream

  • 200g powdered sugar
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 vanilla

Instructions:

Mix warm butter and both types of sugars together. Then add milk. After milk, add eggs and mix. Add baking powder, and vanilla and blend. Lastly, add flour separately. Put the dough in the fridge for an hour. After an hour, take the dough, cut forms you like and put it in the oven at 180 degrees for 10-15 minutes.

Add powdered sugar to egg whites slowly. Then add vanilla, and mix the cream until it's solid. Color the creams and decorate the cookies. Enjoy!
