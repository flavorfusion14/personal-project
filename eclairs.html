Time: 45 minutes
Portion: Serves 20
Difficulty: Medium

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

Dough

  • 250g milk
  • 250g water
  • 250g flour
  • 150g butter
  • A little bit of salt

Cream

  • 70g butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 125g flour
  • 125g milk

Icing

  • 90g dark chocolate
  • 2-3tb/sp milk

Instructions:

For dough, in a boiler put water, milk, salt, and butter. When butter melts add flour. Let it cool down and mix eggs one at a time in the bowl. Put it on the oven pan at 190 degrees for 20-25 minutes.

For cream, in a bowl mix sugar, flour, and boiled milk. On the low gas heat it until it's solid. After that, cool it down and add butter.

For icing, melt chocolate and milk on a low gas. Serve and enjoy!
