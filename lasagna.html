Time: 45 minutes
Portion: Serves 12
Difficulty: Medium

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • 1kg beef
  • 600g mozzarella
  • 70g butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 6tb/sp tomato pasta
  • 1.5 onions
  • Lasagna sheets
  • 6tb/sp flour
  • 800ml milk

Instructions:

For the sauce, melt butter with flour, add milk, and mix until solid. Then fry onion with beef for 15 minutes. Add tomato pasta, salt and black pepper to beef. After these, in the vessel, put a little bit of sauce, lasagna sheets (if it doesn't need any precooking, else boil it for 10 minutes), meat, sauce, and cheese, and repeat this process. Bake it in the oven at 200 degrees for 25 minutes until the cheese on the top is brownish. Serve and enjoy!
