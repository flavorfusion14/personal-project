Time: 20 minutes
Portion: Serves 5
Difficulty: Easy

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • 170g dark chocolate
  • 120g butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 vanilla
  • 2tb/sp flour
  • 4tb/sp sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • Cocoa powder

Instructions:

Melt chocolate and butter over the steam. Let it cool down. Mix sugar and eggs until it's pale. Then add melted chocolate and butter. After that, add flour, salt, and vanilla. In the ramekins, put butter and cocoa powder. Fill the ramekins 3/4. Bake it at 200 degrees for 7-10 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
