Time: 45 minutes
Portion: Serves 12
Difficulty: Easy

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

Dough

  • 2 Eggs
  • 180g Sugar
  • 200ml Milk
  • 50ml Oil
  • 325g Flour
  • 1 Vanilla
  • 1 tb/sp Baking Soda

Cream

  • 200g Flour
  • 180g Sugar
  • 500ml Warm Water
  • 175g Butter
  • 1 Vanilla

Instructions:

For dough, swirl egg whites and sugar. Then add egg yolks and blend. Add milk, oil, and baking soda, swirl, then add vanilla and mix. Lastly, add flour slowly. Bake in the oven, 170 degrees, for 15-20 minutes. For cream, boil sugar, flour, water together on low gas. Cool it down. Blend butter, vanilla, and the mix of sugar, flour and water. Decorate your muffins. Serve and enjoy!
