Time: 25 minutes
Portion: Serves 2
Difficulty: Easy

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato pasta
  • Olive oil
  • 7g Butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Pepper
  • Oregano
  • 200ml whipping cream

Instructions:

Boil the pasta on medium heat for 15 minutes. Add a small amount of olive oil on the pan. Add the tomato pasta and swirl. Add the pasta water which we took from the boiler, and blend. Then, add whipping cream. Blend. Add seasonings: salt, oregano, black and red pepper (optional). Lastly, add butter and blend. Add the sauce to the pasta, add cheese (optional) and enjoy!
