Time: 30 minutes
Portion: Serves 9
Difficulty: Easy

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • 250g mascarpone
  • 2 eggs
  • 250ml whipping cream
  • 1 vanilla
  • 50g sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • 3tb/sp coffee
  • Ladyfingers (18)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Warm water

Instructions:

Separate egg yolks and whites. Take a bowl, blend egg whites until it gets fluffy, then add sugar. Mix well. Take another bowl, pour whipping cream, and blend. After it gets solid, add vanilla. Take another bowl, and gently blend mascarpone. Add whipping cream, egg whites, and lastly salt to mascarpone. Mix coffee in warm water and wet the ladyfingers. In a container (can be glass or plastic) put ladyfingers, add cream, then repeat this process. Lastly, sprinkle the cocoa powder. Put it in the fridge overnight. Enjoy!
