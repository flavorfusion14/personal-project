Time: 25 minutes
Portion: Serves 17
Difficulty: Easy

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 120g sugar
  • 1 vanilla
  • 125g melted butter
  • 250ml milk
  • 250g flour
  • 1tsp baking soda

Instructions:

Blend egg whites with mixer. Then add egg yolks and sugar to it. Mix. After that, add butter and milk. Blend. Add baking soda and vanilla, then flour and mix. Cook it in the toaster. Enjoy!
