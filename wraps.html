Time: 20 minutes
Portion: Serves 6
Difficulty: Easy

Watch the video here

Ingredients:

  • 1kg boiled chicken
  • Dill
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lavash/Tortilaa

Amounts are optional

Instructions:

Fry bell pepper and onion in olive oil with chicken on medium gas. Then in a bowl, add dill, salt, and mayonnaise to the chicken. Lastly, put this mix in lavash and serve. Enjoy!
